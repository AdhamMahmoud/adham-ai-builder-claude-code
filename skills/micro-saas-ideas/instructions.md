## Validation Checklist

### Market
- [ ] Clear target audience (job title/industry)
- [ ] Pain point costs time/money
- [ ] Willing to pay ($20-$100/mo)
- [ ] Can reach via SEO/Twitter/LinkedIn
- [ ] Market size >$100M

### Product
- [ ] Can build MVP in 2-4 weeks
- [ ] Solves ONE problem extremely well
- [ ] Has "aha moment" in first use
- [ ] 10x better than alternatives (speed/price/UX)
- [ ] Low ongoing maintenance

### Business Model
- [ ] Subscription or usage-based pricing
- [ ] $20-$100/mo sweet spot
- [ ] Can validate with landing page
- [ ] Can acquire customers for <$100
- [ ] LTV/CAC ratio > 3

1. Build landing page
2. Set up payments
3. Ship MVP to 10 users
4. Get feedback
5. Iterate
6. Launch on Product Hunt
7. Share on Twitter/LinkedIn
8. SEO content
9. Reach profitability ($1k MRR)

## Red Flags
- ❌ No clear target user
- ❌ Free users won't convert
- ❌ Competing with free tools
- ❌ Requires enterprise sales
- ❌ High churn risk
- ❌ Can't reach users organically
- ❌ Too broad (do everything)

## Success Patterns
- ✅ Vertical-specific tool
- ✅ 10x better on one metric
- ✅ Simple pricing
- ✅ Self-serve onboarding
- ✅ Quick "aha moment"
- ✅ Solves urgent pain
- ✅ Can build alone
